20: [Color:0x5]Press Turn Icons[Color:0x0]
The blue icons in the top right corner of
the top screen are called Press Turn Icons.
They represent the number of actions you
can perform this turn.
[END]
21: In general, the number of icons at the start
is equal to the number of your party members.

[Color:0x5]Flashing Icons[Color:0x0]
When you exploit an enemy's weakness or land
a critical hit, a Press Turn Icon will flash,
signifying that you can take an extra action.

[Color:0x5]Battle Tips[Color:0x0]
Exploiting enemy weaknesses and gaining more 
actions per turn is the key to victory. 
Juggling basic attacks and skills with elemental 
properties such as Ice and Elec will let you hit
multiple enemies' weaknesses.

But just as you can gain actions, you can also
lose them. If your attack misses or is nullified,
you'll lose more than just one Press Turn Icon,
leaving you at a disadvantage.

The same applies to enemies. If an enemy hits
your weakness, they'll gain more actions. If
you dodge their attack or nullify it, however,
the enemy will lose more actions.

This concludes the flow of battle overview.
Keep an eye out for enemy weaknesses, be mindful
of your own, and look for opportunities to turn
the tide of battle in your favor.
[END]

helps make the protagonist stronger. Some demon

When the protagonist already knows a whispered
skill, that skill gains an upgrade. Each skill

[Color:0x5]Demon Skills and Affinities[Color:0x0]
Demon skills cannot be strengthened. Instead,
demons have innate affinities for specific
types of skills. The higher their affinity
is, the better they are at those skills.

If a demon's affinity is at the max
level for that skill type, the number
shown on screen will be yellow.
Conversely, if it is at the lowest
level, the number will be magenta.

You can check a demon's skill affinities on
the status screen.
