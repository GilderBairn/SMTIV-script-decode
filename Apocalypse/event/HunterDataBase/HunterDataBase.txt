[Color:0xc]Becoming a Hunter[Color:0x0]
In order for a cadet to advance to
full Hunter status, they must first
apprentice under an appropriately
ranked Hunter. Following extensive
training, the Hunter must then
recommend the cadet to the Hunter
Association.

Alternatively, if a Hunter application's
achievements and accomplishments can
be assessed as equal or superior to
standard training, they might be given
official Hunter status. Few, however,
have become a Hunter through these
means.

As for the Cadet experience, training
varies greatly between instructors.
There is no formal curriculum. The
work asked of Hunters spans a wide
range of skills, though some Hunters
have been known to specialize.

anthropomorphized versions of natural
phenomena or, in certain cultures,
minions of evil. Presently used to
describe the non−terrestrial life−forms
that appeared after God's Plan. The term
encompasses a variety of creatures across
a spectrum of cultural backgrounds.
[Color:0xc]Races[Color:0x0]
There are over fifty known demon races,
identified by appearance and origin.
[Color:0xc]Emergence[Color:0x0]
Modern reports of demons place their
emergence in the 1980s, though at the
time their presence was so low they
were often dismissed as urban legend.
After the incident with the Yamato
Perpetual Reactor in which a gate to
the Expanse was created, the demon
population surged. Following God's
Plan, reports of humans turning into
demons also began to surface, though
the cause for this remains unknown.
One theory asserts that the magnetite
found naturally in all humans is
somehow able to react to will and
desire, sometimes triggering said
transformation. If true, this theory
would mean that all humans are then
capable of becoming demons. The bulk
of this theory comes from reports
about the human consumption of Reds,
but because evidence of Red−induced
transformation into demons is so rare,
the theory remains speculative at best.

110: [Color:0xc]Summary[Color:0x0]
A cascade of wars and economic collapse
that spread across the world in 2013,
following the Yamato Perpetual Reactor
incident that resulted in the opening
of a gateway to the Expanse. This
incident coincides with the digital
distribution of the Demon Summoning
Program. As a result of both events,
thousands of demons appeared throughout
the world, instigating global chaos.

The ensuing wars would see over a
hundred nuclear missiles launched all
across the world, spread so that no
surface on Earth would be left
unscorched. The wars taxed the global
economy $1.7 trillion dollars and
caused an estimated 7.2 billion
casualties. The exact number remains
unknown.
[Color:0xc]Tokyo During the Missile Launch[Color:0x0]
The worldwide simultaneous launch of
nuclear missiles included Tokyo, but
just before the city was struck, the
dome of rocks now referred to as the
Ceiling appeared, preventing any
damage.
[Color:0xc]Unexplained Mysteries Surrounding God's Plan[Color:0x0]
A great many mysteries persist from
God's Plan. The Demon Summoning Program,
for instance, remains an enigma. Who
created it? Why? Though many theories
exist, a definitive answer has yet to
be found. The details surrounding the
global missile launch also remain unknown.
When the war ended, and the angels
appeared, many ascribed responsibility
to them. This is why the event came to
be known as God's Plan.
[END]
